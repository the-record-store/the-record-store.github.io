The Record Store

If you’re really into old music, you should join my Discord server. We have almost 100 people who know about music. We have staff who make 12 picks each every week. We talk about all kinds of music. 1920’s, 1930’s, 1940’s, 1950’s, 1960’s, 1970’s, 1980’s, 1990’s, early 2000’s and the very few good artists of today. We discuss pretty much all genres of music including but not limited to Rock, Pop, Metal, Classical, Country, Rythm and Blues, Soul, Jazz, Disco, Punk, Electronic, etc. We have dedicated staff who have extensive musical knowledge not only in different genres and eras of music but also in the history of music including the history of individual artists and groups, individual songs and many more things. We are an active community of music enthusiasts of all ages.
